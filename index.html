About

My name is Tina, and I have been reading tarot for over a decade. My favorite cards are Strength and the 3 of cups. When I'm not reading tarot, you can find me refining my software development skills. I personally built this website.

To me, tarot is a tool for reflection and meditation. It can help you learn about yourself, your intentions, your habits, and the mark you leave on the world. When I feel I have taken a backseat in my own life, tarot helps me feel more present, engaged, and decisive. It is my goal to share this empowerment with others. Whether you're a seasoned spiritualist or a keen-eyed skeptic, I invite you to join me in exploring what my tarot readings can offer.
